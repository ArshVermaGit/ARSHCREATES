About Me

Building Interactive Worlds

I'm Arsh Verma, a dedicated creative developer and Computer Science student at VIT Bhopal University, passionate about crafting engaging digital experiences across multiple mediums.

My creative journey encompasses game development, web design, photography, videography, and digital art. This diverse skill set allows me to approach projects from multiple perspectives, creating comprehensive and innovative solutions.

Specializing in Unity and C# for game development, modern web technologies for website creation, and professional photography and video production, I bring ideas to life through carefully crafted experiences. My technical foundation spans JavaScript, Python, HTML/CSS, and advanced post-production techniques.

I'm actively seeking opportunities to collaborate with forward-thinking teams and contribute to groundbreaking projects that push creative boundaries across gaming, web development, visual storytelling, and beyond.
